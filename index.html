About

Our Passionate Team

At J. O'Connor Tennis School, we are dedicated to providing expert instruction to individuals of all ages and skill levels. Our focus on stroke analysis and tactical strategies ensures that our students develop into competent players. We offer a range of services including group lessons, private coaching, and seasonal camps, all aimed at fostering a genuine love for the game in a fun and engaging environment.
